# Wealthwise - Daily Expense Management Platform

## Project Overview

Wealthwise is a MERN stack-based web application that aims to provide a comprehensive daily expense management platform to its users. The application will be designed to cater to the needs of individuals who are looking for an efficient and easy-to-use solution to manage their daily expenses. The application will be of professional complexity, with a robust set of features and functionalities that will enable users to track their expenses, analyze their spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions.

## Domain Context

The financial services domain is one of the most critical areas that require proper management and tracking of expenses. Wealthwise aims to address this need by providing a comprehensive platform that allows users to manage their daily expenses efficiently. The application will be designed to be user-friendly, with an intuitive interface that enables users to quickly navigate through the various features and functionalities.

## Core Features

### Daily Expense Management
The daily expense management feature of Wealthwise will enable users to track their expenses in real-time. Users can enter their expenses manually or import them from their bank accounts. The application will categorize expenses automatically, making it easier for users to analyze their spending patterns. Users can set budgets for different categories and receive alerts when they exceed their limits.

### Dashboard & Visualization
Wealthwise will also provide users with a dashboard that displays an overview of their expenses. The dashboard will include graphs and charts that enable users to visualize their spending patterns. Users can also view their expenses by category, date, and merchant. The application will also provide users with the option to export their expense data to Excel or PDF formats.

### Technology Stack
The MERN stack technology used in the development of Wealthwise will ensure that the application is fast, reliable, and scalable. The application will be built using MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js. These technologies are known for their robustness, flexibility, and ease of use, making them an ideal choice for developing a complex financial services application like Wealthwise.

### Advanced Features
The professional complexity of Wealthwise will be reflected in its advanced features and functionalities. The application will provide users with the option to set financial goals and track their progress. Users can also create custom reports that provide insights into their spending patterns. The application will also include a budget planner that enables users to plan their expenses for the future.

## Conclusion

In conclusion, Wealthwise is a MERN stack-based web application that aims to provide a comprehensive daily expense management platform to its users. The application will be of professional complexity, with a robust set of features and functionalities that will enable users to track their expenses, analyze their spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions. With its user-friendly interface, advanced features, and scalable technology, Wealthwise will be an ideal solution for individuals looking to manage their daily expenses efficiently.
